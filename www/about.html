Hi, my name is Scott

And this the story all about how my life got flipped turned upside down.

I love design. Although I dabble in photography and screen-printing, my true passion is communication design. I currently study Wed Design & New Media at the Academy Of Art in San Francisco. My exposure to the art world started with watercolors and pastels, but I now specialize in UX, UI, web design, identity work, logo design and concepting.

I enjoy creating rich user experiences that feel both emersive and seamless. When designing I pay close attention to my client's brand. I believe that concept is paramount and harmony should always be adheared to. A strong ideation process should always play a key role in every design decision. But before everything else, I always start on paper. Whether it's logo sketches or wireframes, I utilize the tried and true art of pen and paper.

Oh, and my spirt animal is a tauntaun. I hope you enjoy my work!
